FINANCIAL TECHNOLOGY CLUB

AT VIRGINIA TECH

ABOUT US

The Financial Technology Club at Virginia Tech holds member-run workshops aimed at developing specific skills, hosts company recruiters to discuss how they use financial technology at their respective firms, and works on group projects all with the aim of teaching our members valuable technical skills as well as connect them to corporations for possible internships.

OUR MISSION

To create an atmostphere where students with a shared interest in investing, can come to learn more about financial technology, improve their financial skills, and share their financial tips with others. They should also be able to learn about the versatility of fintech and how technology intertwines with so many areas of finance, and with this, we should allow members to learn about a unique and important field in technology.

We will accomplish and abide by our mission statement by inviting guest speakers ranging from business professors to Industry Professionals. Workshops are also another part of our vision as students work with fellow classmates across all majors to perfect their personal portfolios by sharing different financial technology based strategies.
